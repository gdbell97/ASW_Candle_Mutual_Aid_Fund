Aja's Story

The Candle Connoisseur

Meet Aja, our dear friend with an unprecedented passion for Yankee Candles. What started as a simple joy for scented ambiance evolved into something much more profound. Aja doesn't just collect candles; she experiences them in the most literal sense - she has eaten every single one of her beloved Yankee Candles.

In her own words: "There's something about the texture and the lingering taste of those carefully crafted scents that creates an experience unlike any other." While most people simply light their candles, Aja has forged a unique path, redefining what it means to truly appreciate the art of candle-making.

Unfortunately, the Newell Brand's Friends & Family Sale is months away, and Aja's collection has been fully... consumed. This is where our community comes together. Through this mutual aid fund, we aim to help Aja rebuild her candle collection, ensuring she continues to enjoy her unusual but heartfelt passion.

Every donation helps bring a new candle into Aja's life, illuminating her world in more ways than one.
